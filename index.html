Dr. Tara Kapoor

With over 8 years of surgical experience, Dr. Kapoor has built her practice on a single principle: treat every patient the way you would treat family. Her philosophy centres on conservative, evidence-based surgery — always choosing the least invasive option that delivers the best long-term outcome.

Her training at AIIMS and fellowship in laparoscopic surgery give her a rare combination of technical precision and genuine compassion. Dr. Kapoor believes that an informed patient is an empowered patient.

A Thoughtful Approach to Surgery

At Dr. Tara Kapoor's practice, every treatment begins with understanding. We believe in conservative, evidence-based surgery — preserving as much of your natural anatomy as possible and intervening only when truly necessary.

Before any procedure, you will receive a clear explanation of your diagnosis, all available treatment options, and our honest recommendation. There is no rush, no pressure, and no unnecessary procedures.

Our commitment to comfort extends beyond the operating room. From the calm atmosphere of our clinic to our rigorous sterilisation protocols, every detail is designed to make your experience reassuring and dignified.

We focus on long-term surgical wellness — building a relationship of trust that helps you maintain your health for life.

What We Offer

Surgical Care Offered

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive keyhole surgery for gallbladder, appendix, hernia and abdominal conditions — less pain, faster recovery.

General & Emergency Surgery

Comprehensive surgical care for acute abdominal emergencies including appendicitis, bowel obstruction, and trauma.

Hernia Repair

State-of-the-art mesh and laparoscopic repair for inguinal, umbilical, incisional and abdominal wall hernias.

Colorectal Surgery

Specialised management of colorectal conditions including cancer, diverticular disease, and inflammatory bowel disease.

Bariatric Surgery

Sleeve gastrectomy and gastric bypass with comprehensive pre- and post-operative multidisciplinary support.

Oncological Surgery

Surgical oncology for thyroid, adrenal and soft-tissue tumours with margin-negative precision and cancer board collaboration.

Second Opinion

Unbiased, expert review of your diagnosis and proposed treatment plan — because clarity before surgery matters.

Pre & Post-Op Care

Dedicated pre-surgical preparation and personalised recovery protocols for the smoothest possible outcome.
